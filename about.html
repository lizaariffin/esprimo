ABOUT

Esprimo

Established in 2010, Esprimo has extensive experience in providing events management and consultancy services. In the face of the Covid-19 pandemic, we also stepped up to conduct safe management operations for events and various sites. Starting from the year 2021, we will also be engaged in Facilities Management, adding to the spectrum of services that we provide. Esprimo prides itself in its service excellence and high esprit de corps. We seek to deliver the best solutions to our customers. You can rest assured that our service will be of the highest standard and tailored to your objectives and needs.

Esprimo (M&E) Pte Ltd.

Esprimo (M&E) Pte Ltd, a subsidiary of Esprimo Pte Ltd, a business with more than 10 years of experience providing Lighting Consultancy and Electrical Installation services to clients in both the Government and Private sectors. Our Team are passionate, dedicated and we are committed in providing and supporting our valuable Clients with strategic approach and planning, to meet their requirements at their best value without compromising quality and safety
